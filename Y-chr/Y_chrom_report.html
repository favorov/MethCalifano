Trying to test which measure feels better that chrY methylation is represented in female samples less than in male ones

Alexander Favorov

1 Mar 2014

We will test three measures: the coverage of methylation MACS peaks, their score (summed over chromosome) and their product. To do this, we use an absurd comparison of methylation of Y-chromosome between males and females.

We will make Wilcoxon test for the Y-chr methylation mesure levels and we will make a test of 1's for all males against 0's for all females to have a watermark (the last comparison).
